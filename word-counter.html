What is the ExploreaToolHub Word Counter?

Our Word Counter is a professional, free online tool designed for writers, students, and professionals who need accurate text statistics. It works instantly in your browser to count words, characters, and sentences as you type. With no signup required and a focus on privacy, it is the fastest way to check your text length online.

How to use this tool?

  • Paste the text into the large workspace above.
  • View the live statistics as they update with every keystroke.
  • Adjust your content to meet specific word count requirements.
